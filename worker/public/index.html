One card per line as <count> <card name>; a Sideboard line splits maindeck from sideboard. The count sets a nominal (how many the reference list runs — every deck's diff is measured from it) and an allowed range. A card whose count stays inside its range still shows its delta but isn't flagged as tech:
  • 4 Lightning Bolt — exactly 4 (nominal 4, no tolerance).
  • 2-3 Mountain — allowed 2–3; nominal is the low end (2).
  • 10 (8-10) Fetchland — nominal 10, allowed 8–10.
Nominals should sum to 60 maindeck / 15 sideboard so the columns balance, and all fetchlands share the single Fetchland line.
